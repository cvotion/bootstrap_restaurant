Welcome to Carmelo’s Mexican Grill!

Home of the Mucho Grande Burrito! Carmelo’s Burritos and Tacos offers the best in authentic Mexican food in Sugar Land, TX.

Our Mexican food is hand-made from only the finest, freshest ingredients. Whether you’re in the mood for some mouth-watering fajitas, nachos, a burrito so big it takes up your entire plate, or an ice-cold margarita, we’ve got it. Need something delicious to start your day off with? We’ve got the best breakfast tacos and burritos around.


Always Fresh
Everything we make is prepared especially for you. Every day we make all our salsa’s fresh – not out of a bottle and our chips are made fresh, not out of a bag. We use fresh vegetables and prime meats to make your meal. You can taste the difference.
